HTML Entities

HTML entities are special characters used to represent symbols or characters that have special meaning in HTML.
They start with an ampersand(&) and with a semicolon(;).

    Currency Symbols
  • Dollar $
  • Euro €
  • Yen ¥
  • Pound £
  • Cent ¢

  • Degree Symbol °
  • TradeMark Symbol ™
